Typography

Cormorant Garamond for headlines — a modern serif with old-soul warmth. Montserrat for labels — clean, architectural, precise.

Generous margins. Breathing room between elements. White space as a luxury good — the design equivalent of a spacious room.

Radial gradients that mimic gallery lighting. Subtle illumination from a single source. The page glows, it does not shout.
