三 / San
古池や
蛙飛び込む
水の音

古池。青蛙跃入。水声。十七个音节。一个宇宙。这就是编辑原则：每个元素都必须承载重量。不能加深意义，就删掉。

Bashō's frog pond. Seventeen syllables. An entire universe. This is the editorial principle: every element must carry weight. If it doesn't deepen the meaning, remove it.

七 / Shichi
間

Ma — 留白

乐音之间的停顿。茶室的空墙。空间不是剩余物——它是构图的主要材料。

The pause between notes. The empty wall in a tea room. Space is not what's left over — it is the primary material of composition.

深

Fukami — 深度

意义随时间逐层揭示，而非一次性全部展示。滚动触发层层渐现。页面在呼吸。

Layers of meaning revealed through time, not all at once. Scroll-triggered reveals. Progressive disclosure. The page breathes.

寂

Sabi — 寂之美

岁月的美。柔和的色调。纹理背景。一个有生活痕迹的设计。Wabi-sabi 作为 UX。

The beauty of age. Muted colors. Textured backgrounds. A design that feels lived-in, not freshly minted. Wabi-sabi as UX.

寂び

静けさの中に
真実がある

寂静之中，真实存在。禅的暗色模式。墨汁在和纸上。高对比不是为了吸引注意力，而是为了清晰。最终还原：黑与白。

In silence, truth. The dark mode of zen. Sumi ink on washi paper. High contrast not for attention, but for clarity. The ultimate reduction: black and white.

五 / Go

非対称
Asymmetry

日本构图偏好偏离中心。奇数。不平衡的平衡。插花中一枝略微偏斜。网格中刻意打破。完美是死的，不对称是活的。

Japanese composition favors the off-center. The odd number. A flower arrangement with one stem slightly askew. Perfection is sterile; asymmetry is alive.
